## Component Design System

### **Button Styles**
- Primary: `#4F46E5` (Indigo) - Main CTAs
- Secondary: `#10B981` (Emerald) - Secondary actions
- Outline: Transparent with border - Less prominent actions

### **Card Styles**
- Default: White background, subtle shadow, rounded corners
- Hover: Slightly darker background, increased shadow
- Active: Border highlight, distinct from others

### **Typography**
- Headers: Bold, clear hierarchy
- Body: Readable, appropriate line height
- Numbers: Monospace for metrics/data

### **Color Scheme**
- Primary: Indigo (#4F46E5)
- Success: Emerald (#10B981)
- Warning: Amber (#F59E0B)
- Error: Rose (#E11D48)
- Neutral: Slate (#64748B)
