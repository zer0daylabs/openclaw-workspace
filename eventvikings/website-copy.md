### **FAQ Section**

**Headline**: **Frequently Asked Questions**

**Q**: What makes EventVikings different from other sponsorship platforms?
**A**: Unlike traditional platforms, we're built around measurable ROI. Every sponsorship includes real-time analytics, lead generation tools, and comprehensive reporting so you can prove the value of your investment.

**Q**: Can I customize a sponsorship package?
**A**: Absolutely! We offer à la carte options and work with each sponsor to create a package that aligns with their specific goals. Our team will help you identify the best combination of activations.

**Q**: What's included in the analytics dashboard?
**A**: You'll get live tracking of impressions, engagement rates, leads generated, QR code scans, social media mentions, booth traffic, conversion rates, and a calculated ROI. Reports can be exported in PDF or CSV format.

**Q**: How long before an event do I need to commit?
**A**: We recommend at least 4-6 weeks for standard packages, with assets due 2 weeks before. However, we do have some last-minute opportunities - contact us to see what's available.

**Q**: Can I track my ROI in real-time?
**A**: Yes! Our dashboard provides live data from day one. You'll see impressions, leads, and engagement as they happen throughout the event.

**Q**: What industries do you work with?
**A**: We work with a wide range of industries including technology, healthcare, sports, entertainment, conferences, and community events. The key is finding the right audience match.

**Q**: Do you offer white-label options for large organizers?
**A**: Yes, for enterprise sponsors and large event organizers, we offer white-label sponsorship solutions. Contact our sales team for details.
