## 🎯 Quick Start - Where to Find Venues

### **Primary Directories:**

**1. EventUp.com** ⭐ TOP PICK
- URL: https://eventup.com/
- Coverage: All major US cities + Canada
- Venues: 300,000+
- Features: Availability, pricing, reviews, direct contact
- Best for: Parties, weddings, corporate meetings

**2. VenueDir.com**
- URL: https://www.venuedir.com/
- Coverage: US + International
- Venues: 300,000+
- Features: City-based search, room layout filters
- Best for: Meetings, events, conferences

**3. Eventective.com**
- URL: https://www.eventective.com/usa/
- Coverage: US + Canada
- Features: Weddings, parties, meetings
- Best for: Social events, weddings, corporate

- URL: https://conventionsouth.com/directory-online/

- Best for: Corporate events, conventions

**5. Bizbash.com Venue Directory**
- URL: https://www.bizbash.com/venue-directory
- Coverage: National (corporate focus)
- Features: Corporate event venues
- Best for: Business meetings, conferences

## 📧 Venue Outreach Email Template

### **Initial Contact Email:**

```
Subject: Event Inquiry - [Event Type] - [Date(s)]

Dear [Venue Name] Events Team,

I hope this email finds you well. My name is [Your Name], and I am reaching out from [Your Organization] to inquire about venue options for an upcoming event.

**Event Details:**
- Event Type: [Corporate Meeting/Wedding/Conference/Party/etc.]
- Proposed Dates: [Date(s) or flexible dates]
- Expected Attendees: [Number]
- Duration: [Hours/Days]
- Location Preference: [Specific city/area or flexible]

**Requirements:**
- Capacity: [Minimum - Maximum attendees]
- Space Needed: [Ballroom, conference rooms, breakout spaces, outdoor area, etc.]
- A/V Equipment: [List specific needs]
- Catering: [In-house or external allowed]
- Parking: [Number of spaces needed]
- Special Needs: [Any accessibility or other requirements]

**Budget:**
[Optional: Include budget range or "Looking for competitive quote"]

Could you please provide:
1. Availability for our proposed dates
2. Rental rates and what's included
3. Catering options (if applicable)
4. Any additional fees (service charges, taxes, etc.)
5. Cancellation policy

I would appreciate the opportunity to schedule a site visit at your earliest convenience. Please let me know what dates and times work best for you.

Thank you for your consideration. I look forward to hearing from you soon.

Best regards,

[Your Full Name]  
[Your Title]  
[Your Organization]  
[Your Phone Number]  
[Your Email Address]
```

## 📊 Venue Research Checklist

### **Before Contacting:**

- [ ] Verify venue type matches event needs
- [ ] Check capacity requirements
- [ ] Confirm location accessibility
- [ ] Review available dates
- [ ] Research price range
- [ ] Note special features
- [ ] Check reviews/reputation

### **During Site Visit:**

- [ ] Inspect main event space
- [ ] Check A/V capabilities
- [ ] Verify parking availability
- [ ] Meet with event coordinator
- [ ] Tour catering facilities
- [ ] Check restrooms/accessibility
- [ ] Note setup/teardown times
- [ ] Ask about backup plans (power, weather)

### **Contract Review:**

- [ ] Clear rental fee structure
- [ ] Deposit amount and due date
- [ ] Cancellation policy
- [ ] Insurance requirements
- [ ] Liability coverage
- [ ] Force majeure clause
- [ ] Payment schedule
- [ ] Staffing included
- [ ] Extra fees and taxes
- [ ] Setup/teardown windows
