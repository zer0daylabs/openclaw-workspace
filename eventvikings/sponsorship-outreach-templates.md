This document contains pre-written email templates for prospecting sponsors, following up with prospects, and nurturing existing sponsors for renewal.

## Best Practices for Outreach

### **Timing**
- Initial outreach: 4-6 months before event
- Follow-up: 5-7 days after initial email
- Re-engagement: 2-3 months before event
- Last-minute: 2-3 weeks before event

### **Personalization**
- Always customize subject line
- Reference their company/recent news
- Connect sponsorship to their specific goals
- Mention past interactions if applicable

### **Follow-Up Strategy**
- Don't give up after one attempt
- Vary messaging in follow-ups
- Provide clear value in each email
- Include clear call-to-action

### **Tracking**
- Track open rates and response rates
- A/B test subject lines
- Monitor which templates work best
- Adjust approach based on results

### **Tools**
- Use email tracking software
- Schedule emails for optimal timing
- Automate follow-up sequences
- Use CRM to track all interactions
