# Partner Prospect Research and Outreach

## Objective
Identify potential sponsors and partners that would benefit from a sponsorship platform integrated with event ticketing. These prospects will be targeted for outreach and pilot testing.

## Target Industries
| Industry | Typical Sponsors | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Beverage & Alcohol | Brewery, Spirits, Non‑Alcoholic Beverage | Strong brand affinity with events, high ROI from sponsorships |
| Technology & SaaS | CRM, marketing automation, event software | Looking for brand exposure and lead generation |
| Hospitality & Restaurants | Local restaurants, hotels | Desire for local exposure and event tie‑ins |
| Finance & Insurance | Banks, credit cards, insurance | Need to reach specific demographic segments |
| Automotive | Car dealers, parts suppliers | Brand visibility at high‑traffic events |
| Apparel & Lifestyle | Fashion brands, athletic wear | Opportunity for product placement and retail sales |
| Entertainment | Music labels, streaming services, film studios | Aligns with event content and audience |

## Potential Partners
1. **SponsorCX** – Offers sponsorship management and integrates with CRMs. Ideal for early adopters.
2. **Trak.io** – Provides real‑time sponsorship metrics, can integrate with EventVikings for ROI dashboards.
3. **ActiveCampaign** – Marketing automation that can push sponsor marketing to event registrants.
4. **Bizzabo** – Event platform with sponsorship features; could partner for co‑marketing.
5. **Bigin** – CRM templates for sponsorship; potential integration or white‑label.

## Outreach Strategy
1. **Segment** prospects by industry and size.
2. **Personalized Outreach**: Use templates created in `sponsorship-outreach-templates.md`.
3. **Value Proposition**: Highlight integrated ticketing + sponsorship, real‑time ROI tracking, flexible tiering.
4. **Follow‑Up**: Track responses in a shared spreadsheet (Google Sheet or Airtable).

## Metrics to Track
- Email open rates
- Response rates
- Meetings booked
- Sponsorship deals closed
